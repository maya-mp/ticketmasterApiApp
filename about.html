What is Event Finder?

With our app you can:

  • Find events meeting your criteria
  • Explore opportunities you may have never looked into
  • Have a centralized place for ticket information

How to Use Our App

Start on the home page with the "Filters" section to select the things you want to narrow your search down by. That will populate the second form to fill, and submission of that form takes you to a chart of a few randomly picked events matching your criteria! Options to explore these events and their details in depth are available a seperate page.

Ticketmaster API


Ticketmaster's Discovery API allows users to filter and find events by searching attractions, events, or venues. Our app uses the event finding endpoint and uses the results in conjunction with the endpoint to get in depth details on each event.
